the beasts tentacles are flailing wildly enough that you could catch yourself on one and begin hacking off the appendages one by one. or, maybe if you cut one, the sharks would come and finish it off.

you watch, waiting for the perfect opportunity to strike. the shouts of your crewmates around you, fighting to defend their home made your courage build up in you and you caught sight of one tentacle that was slower than the others. it lagged behind and had trouble latching onto anything.

before you can talk yourself out of it, you launch yourself off the boat and slice into the tentacle not even a quarter of the way down. the kraken barely notices as you latch yourself onto the flailing appendage and begin wedging the tip of your sword between the scales of the beast to cut it down at the source.

the kraken flails once more, and you are flung up into the air, landing miraculously on its head. your breath is knocked from your lungs, but you don't have the luxury to recover. you roll and pitch your sword into the kraken's soft craniun.

it has the effect you want. the beast spasms and you are nearly thrown from the beast again, but you hold tight to the sword, your right arm wrenching out of its socket.
